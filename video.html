Screen Recorder Features

Use your browser to record anything

Record your screen, webcam video, browser tab, or application right from your browser with Panopto's versatile online screen recorder. There are no recording time limitations, trial periods, watermarks, or feature limitations. Record as much and as many times as you like.

Record multiple sources at the same time

With Panopto Express you can capture and share the most immersive viewing experience. Record any combination of multiple webcams, video capture devices, computer screens, browser tabs, or applications. Panopto automatically merges all sources into a single recording.

Turn any webcam into a Smart Camera

With Panopto Express, using just a standard webcam, you can effortlessly record yourself moving about the room. Smart Camera technology automatically pans, zooms, and follows you within your video frame using artificial intelligence.

Instantly share screen recordings any way you prefer

With Panopto Express, you can instantly share screen and video recordings using YouTube, Google Classroom, or Panopto. You can also download recordings in MP4 format and use with other streaming services and editing software.
